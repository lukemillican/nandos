Our Story

One night at dinner we were discussing our desire for a delicious hot donut! After research showed there were no quality donut shops within a 10 mile radius of Chapel Hill we have a feeling - something must be done. If it wasn’t us, who would? That night at dinner we decided to open up a donut “shop.” In search of giving the people the glaze they deserve. There are few things better than a warm donut on a Sunday, and we know even fewer do it better than we do!

We are three men from Cincinnati who want to create community and spread smiles through a simple donut.
